Have lots of posts linking to Flickr photos? Flickr Media Scanner scans all your posts to find Flickr photos that are no longer available. 

== Description ==

Flickr Media Scanner scans all your posts, looking for Flickr photos that are no longer available, so you can replace them. By default it scans all posts in your site, but you can choose to scan posts from a certain category or posts with certain tags.

== Installation ==

1. Upload Flickr Media Scanner to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. In the preferences menu you will find a new item call `Flickr Media Scanner`
